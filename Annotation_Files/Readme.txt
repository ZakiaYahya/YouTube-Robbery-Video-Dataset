                                                   ** ANNOTATIONS **

-> **Classification Task:** A single label is assigned to a whole video either 1 (robbery) or 2 (norobbery) depending upon the type of                                 activity it contain.
-> **Localization Task:**  Each individual video is splitted into 3-seconds clips and a single label is assigned to each clip instead of                              a video depending upon the activity it comprises.

-> In both Classification and Localization tasks, label 2 indicates NoRobbery/Normal activity while label 1 specifies
   Robbery/Violent/Abnormal activity.
